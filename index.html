About Me

Background & Expertise

With over 8+ years of experience implementing Microsoft Dynamics 365 solutions, I specialise in aligning complex business requirements with the right technology. I have led end-to-end ERP and CRM deployments across finance, supply chain, and customer engagement modules for clients ranging from mid-market enterprises to global organisations.

My approach is rooted in understanding the why before the how — working closely with stakeholders to map processes, identify inefficiencies, and design solutions that genuinely fit the way teams work. I hold Microsoft certifications across Dynamics 365 Finance, Supply Chain Management, and the Power Platform.

Outside of implementation projects I enjoy mentoring junior consultants, contributing to the D365 community, and keeping up with the fast-moving Microsoft release wave cycle.
